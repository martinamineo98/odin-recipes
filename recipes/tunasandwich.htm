Tuna Sandwich

Imagine this is a tuna sandwich, I could not find a better image on Unsplash. Please do not laugh. Thank you.

Description

A dish that even I cannot screw up and it is also very cheap.

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Can of Tuna
  • Mayo
  • Sandwich

Steps

  • Step 1: Boil an egg for 8 minutes. Remove the shell and put it aside in a bowl.
  • Step 2: Open a can of tuna, remove the oil or water, add the tuna in the bowl with the egg.
  • Step 3: Add enough mayo to the bowl, your tuna salad needs to be creamy.
  • Step 4: Put your tuna salad inside your sandwich.
