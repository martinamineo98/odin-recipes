Ragù Sauce

Description

Ragù Sauce is a tomato-based sauced that consists in ground beef, pancetta, carrots, golden onions and celery (or soffritto).

Ingredients

  • 300gr of Ground Beef
  • 50gr of Carrots
  • 50gr of Golden Onions
  • 50gr of Celery
  • Pancetta (or something similar like Bacon, if it is not available in your area)
  • Extra Virgin Oil
  • Bullion Cubes or Vegetable Broth
  • Red Wine
  • Salt
  • Pepper Optional

Steps

  • Step 1: Cook your vegetable broth and keep it warm.
  • Step 2: Cut your pancetta.
  • Step 3: Cut your carrots, onions, celery and keep them aside.
  • Step 4: Put some oil and your pancetta on a saucepan, let it cook for a few minutes. Add your vegetables, after a few minutes add your ground beef, turn up the flame and let it cook for a bit.
  • Step 5: Add your salt and pepper.
  • Step 6: Pour some red wine and, after the wine is gone, add your sauce. When it started boiling again, add your broth slowly.
  • Step 7: Let the ragù cook for at least 2 hours.
