Lasagna alla Bolognese

Description

Bolognese Lasagna is a typical Italian dish generally cooked on Sundays. It originated in the city of Bologna, in Emilia. It consists of layers of pasta, covered in ragù, béchamel and grated cheese sprinkled on top.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of Oven-Ready Lasagne or Homemade Lasagne
  • 300gr of Grated Cheese

  • Ragù Sauce

    • 300gr of Ground Beef
    • 50gr of Carrots
    • 50gr of Golden Onions
    • 50gr of Celery
    • One Bottle of Tomato Sauce
    • Pancetta (or something similar like Bacon, if it is not available in your area)
    • Extra Virgin Oil
    • Bullion Cubes or Vegetable Broth
    • Red Wine
    • Salt
    • Pepper Optional

  • Béchamel

    • 100gr of Butter (and some additional butter to grease your baking tray)
    • 1ltr of Milk (Whole Milk possibly)
    • Nutmeg Optional
    • Salt
    • 100gr of Flour

Steps

  • Ragù Sauce

    • Step 1: Cook your vegetable broth and keep it warm.
    • Step 2: Cut your pancetta.
    • Step 3: Cut your carrots, onions, celery and keep them aside.
    • Step 4: Put some oil and your pancetta on a saucepan, let it cook for a few minutes. Add your vegetables, after a few minutes add your ground beef, turn up the flame and let it cook for a bit.
    • Step 5: Add your salt and pepper.
    • Step 6: Pour some red wine and, after the wine is gone, add your sauce. When it started boiling again, add your broth slowly.
    • Step 7: Let the ragù cook for at least 2 hours.

  • Bèchamel

    • Step 1: In a saucepan, heat up your whole milk.
    • Step 2: In another saucepan, melt your butter.
    • Step 3: Add your flour in the butter. Cook it for a few minutes.
    • Step 4: Pour your warm whole milk into the butter, remember to stir it.
    • Step 5: Add your salt and your nutmeg, keep stirring it until the béchamel is creamy.

  • Building your Lasagne

    • Step 1: Take a big baking tray and grease it with butter.
    • Step 2: Add a thin layer of béchamel and a layer of ragù.
    • Step 3: Add your first Lasagna Noodle.
    • Step 4: Repeat 'Step 2' and 'Step 3', untill you ran out of noodles.
    • Step 5: Add a layer of béchamel and cover it with grated cheese.
    • Step 6: Cook your lasagnas in a preheated oven at 170°C for about 40 minutes.
